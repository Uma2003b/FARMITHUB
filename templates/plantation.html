Irrigation After Planting

üíß Watering Schedule

  • First watering: Immediately after sowing (light spray)
  • Germination phase: Keep soil moist for 7-10 days
  • Seedling stage: Water every 2-3 days
  • Growth stage: Water based on rainfall and soil moisture
  • Flowering: Increase watering frequency
  • Fruiting: Maintain consistent moisture

üö∞ Irrigation Methods

  • Flood irrigation: Traditional, uses more water
  • Drip irrigation: Efficient, saves 40-60% water
  • Sprinkler: Good for vegetables and pulses
  • Furrow: Suitable for row crops
  • Rainfed: Rely on natural rainfall

Fertilization After Planting

üå± Nutrient Management

  • Basal dose: Apply at sowing time (compost + NPK)
  • Top dressing: Apply nitrogen at 30-45 days
  • Foliar spray: Micronutrients at critical stages
  • Organic: Use vermicompost and biofertilizers
  • Timing: Apply after rain or irrigation
